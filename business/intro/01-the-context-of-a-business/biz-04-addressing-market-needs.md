## Key Points

1.  A market is one of many varieties of systems, institutions, procedures, social relations, and infrastructures whereby parties engage in exchange.
2.  Many companies today have a customer focus (or market orientation). This implies that the company focuses its activities and products on consumer demands.
3.  Market research is for discovering what people want, need, and believe; and how they behave.
4.  Market segmentation is the division of the market or population into subgroups with similar motivations.
