## Evaluating Expressions with Two Variables

**Example 1**

Given $a=7,b=2$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& a + b \\
=\, & 7 + 2 \\
=\, & 9
\end{aligned}
$$

**Example 2**

Given $x=3,y=2$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& xy - y + 3x \\
=\, & (3)(2) - (2) + 3(3) \\
=\, & 6 - 2 + 9 \\
=\, & 13
\end{aligned}
$$
