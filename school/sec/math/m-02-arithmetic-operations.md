## Order of Operations

1. PEMA rule

    | Order | Operations | Variation |
    |:---:|:---|:---|
    | 1 | **P**arenthesis $()$ | |
    | 2 | **E**xponent $(a^x)$ | $(a^{\frac{1}{x}})$ or $\sqrt[x]{a}$ |
    | 3 | **M**ultiplication $(a\times x)$ | Division $(a\times \frac{1}{x})$ or $(a\div x)$ |
    | 4 | **A**ddition $(a+x)$ | Subtraction $(a+(-x))$ or $(a-x)$ |
