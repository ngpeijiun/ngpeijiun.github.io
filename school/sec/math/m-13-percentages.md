# Percentages

*Apr 17, 2025*

## Percentage Basics

1.  Calculate a given percentage of a quantity.

    -   Method: Multiply the quantity by the percentage (expressed as a decimal).
    -   Example:

        Find $20\%$ of $80$:

        $$
        80\times0.2=16
        $$
