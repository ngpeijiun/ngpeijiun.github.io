## Probability

1. Probability is the chance of an event happening.

    $$
    \text{probability of an event to happen} =\frac{\text{number of favourable outcomes}}{\text{total number of outcomes}}
    $$

2. Probability ranges between $0$ and $1$, where $0$ is the probability of something impossible to happen and $1$ is the probability of something certain to happen.
