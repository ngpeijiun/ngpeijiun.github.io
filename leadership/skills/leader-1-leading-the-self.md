## Automatic Cycle of Behaviour

1.  Automaticity is a very important aspect about our behaviour. We are all in our work life and also personal lives, we are bombarded with event situations that we encounter. If we are not careful, we act in an automatic manner and that is the principle of automaticity in psychology in organisation behaviour.

2.  Let us imagine a situation at office. You are working in office on a project, and the boss comes to you and tells you that you are being moved out of a project. Now, when this situation occurs, then this event occurs in front of you, there are bound to be some thoughts that come to us automatically. Why did the boss ask me to be moved out of a project? Why am I being moved out of this particular project? Is there something wrong in my performance? Is there some problem with how I'm working?

    These thoughts give rise to certain emotions. I may feel sad, I may feel fearful, I may also feel anger. When we have experienced these thoughts and emotions, we are also then bound to take certain actions, which then lead to another set of events and situations that unfold and that's the whole idea of automaticity.
